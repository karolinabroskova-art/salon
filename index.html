About

Hello, I'm Karolina

I am a Slovak in Madrid, a compliance lawyer, a coach in formation, and the founder of Mindsalon. For most of my career, I worked in rooms where words carried legal weight. What I gradually discovered is that the words which actually change people have almost nothing to do with law — they carry emotion, and it is emotion that moves us.

That discovery is what Mindsalon exists to honour. Whether in an intimate salon gathering or a one-to-one coaching conversation, the work is the same: to create the conditions where something real can be said — and something real can shift.

I am still becoming. This is where I invite you to join me.

The Salon

Intimate Gatherings.
Lasting Connections.

Monthly salon evenings in private homes in Madrid — a living revival of one of history's most generative social institutions. Six participants. One pre-selected theme. Moderated dialogue. Real encounter.

01

Curated Intimacy

Each salon welcomes no more than six participants — an intentional constraint that creates genuine depth, ensures every voice is heard, and allows true intellectual and emotional presence.

02

Guided Dialogue

Every gathering is anchored by a pre-selected theme — from philosophy and identity to creativity and the future of human connection — explored through thoughtful, facilitated conversation.

03

Private & Personal

Held in the warmth of private homes, not institutional spaces. The domestic setting is deliberate — it signals trust, lowers defenses, and invites the kind of openness that public venues rarely allow.

04

Cross-Disciplinary

People come from varied generations, nationalities, and professions. The salon thrives on this diversity — artists sit beside engineers, philosophers beside entrepreneurs, seekers beside sceptics.

05

Monthly Rhythm

A new theme, a new gathering, every month. Each evening stands on its own — yet together they form a living, evolving intellectual community in Madrid.

06

In English. Mostly.

Salons are held in English — the natural home of Madrid's international crowd. In practice, the odd Spanish word occasionally makes its way in, and nobody objects. Karolina understands enough to follow along, and enough to know when the conversation gets interesting.

"A salon is like a deep-tissue massage for the mind — it works out the knots that ordinary life leaves behind."

Coaching

Transformative
1:1 Coaching

My coaching practice is grounded in the same philosophy as the salon: that the right conversation, held in the right conditions, can change everything. I work with individuals at genuine inflection points — people who sense that something important needs to shift but aren't yet sure what, or how.

My approach is human-centric, inquiry-led, and deeply respectful of your own wisdom. I don't provide answers; I ask the questions that help you find your own — and hold space for the answers that truly belong to you.

I am currently a coach in formation — bringing to each session not only rigorous training but also the particular attentiveness of someone who is still deepening their craft with genuine curiosity and humility.

Sessions are conducted in English, Czech and Slovak, in person in Madrid or online worldwide.

Deep Work

Coaching Journey

A structured series of 6–12 sessions over three to six months. Together we work through a theme that matters deeply to you, building real clarity, capacity, and change over time.

Collective

Group Coaching

Small-group coaching in the spirit of the salon — combining the intimacy of individual attention with the generative power of shared reflection and peer wisdom.

Themes & Topics

Areas of Exploration

The questions that animate both the salon and the coaching room — chosen not because they are easy, but because they are essential.

01

Identity & Self-Discovery

Who are you beneath the roles, expectations, and stories you've inherited? What becomes possible when you meet yourself honestly?

02

Relationships & Intimacy

How do we build depth, trust, and real connection — in love, friendship, and professional life — in an age of distraction?

03

Meaning & Purpose

What gives your life weight and direction? How do we live with intention rather than just by default?

04

Creativity & Expression

Where does your creativity live, and what is keeping it quiet? Exploring the relationship between expression, courage, and identity.

05

Life Transitions

The crossroads between who you have been and who you are becoming. How to navigate change with clarity, courage, and grace.

06

Communication & Presence

The art of being truly heard — and truly listening. How we speak, how we silence, and what we make possible when we do both well.

07

Human Connection

Belonging, loneliness, and the paradox of our hyperconnected yet isolated age. What does it take to truly meet another person?

08

Inner Dialogue & Mindset

The stories we tell ourselves — about capability, worth, and possibility. Examining and rewriting the narratives that shape our lives.
